  • WHAT IS HEY-CHAIN?

    The beautiful chain Healthbeauty-Chain, or HEY-Chain, is a decentralized medical-aesthetic ecosystem designed to establish a globalized, decentralized, cross-platform medical asset with the uniform value of assets and high liquidity medical beauty group so as to provide a safe and convenient customer information management channel for the medical organization in the world. HEY-Chain focuses on reshaping the value chain of medical beauty industry, creating the ecology of blockchain for the traditional medical beauty industry, and providing the basic support for the entire blockchain medical beauty industry.

  • MEDICAL BEAUTY INDUSTRY WITH RAPID GROWTH

    In the era of competing physical attractiveness, the medical beauty industry is increasing popular in the society. Authoritative data show that medical and beauty industry services and product revenue reached 53.3 billion US dollars in 2017. The next five years is expected to rise with CAGR of 5.5%. In 2022, the market will reach 72.3 billion US dollars. One-third of the future Fortune 500 companies will be engaged in medical beauty technology research, production and sales.However, the development of the medical and beauty industry is not optimistic. Fake drugs, fake doctors and fake institutions. The phenomenon has become the industry criticism, and the price is deceived consumers through implicit packaging. In addition, this industry also exists non-standardization a high degree of opaque, difficult to protect the interests of consumers and other issues.

  • "BLOCKCHAIN + MEDICAL BEAUTY" BRIGHT PROSPECTS

    Blockchain technology can realize decentralized management, irrevocable tracking audit, data traceability, reliable and easy-to-use, privacy and security functions in data processing. Applying it to medical and beauty industry can providing safe and convenient channels of customer information management, which will surely bring about a huge industry innovation and create the next blue ocean for the medical beauty market.

  • MISSION & VISION

    Medical plastic surgery industry needs reform, our vision is to use blockchain technology to promote the progress of the industry. With the blockchain, solving problems from drug delivery, drug distribution, Drug circulation, registration of practitioners’ qualification certificate, the registration of medical institutions qualifications to data recording of the entire operation, traceability of the origin of the problem through decentralized approach to prevent the industry from growing brutally.

  • HEY-CHAIN BUSINESS SCENE APPLICATION EXAMPLES

    1. 1、How to choose the formal medical plastic surgery institutions and physicians?

      Consumers can check registered practitioners with a certificate of qualification and regular medical institutions with medical qualifications in HEY-chain. Documents and qualifications in the chain can ensure reliable through the cooperation with the regulatory agencies to ensure authentic and reliable, and the data cannot be tampered with.

    2. 2、How to ensure formal plastic medicine without problems?

      Healthbeauty will work with global medical plastic surgery companies to ensure that medicines in HEY's cooperative ecosystem are in our chain from the source. Pharmacists, wholesalers, and plastic surgery organizations all can document drug shipments on the blockchain, which means that at every step of the distribution process, the computer network can prove the origin and authenticity of drug shipments, which make medicine theft and fake for true more difficult.

    3. 3、How to ensure the medical effect of plastic surgery?

      HEY chain applications will develop plastic surgery prediction system. Customers can customize their own plastic effect through the system, and compare their own medical effects by the hash record to evaluate their plastic effect. And it can achieve decentralized applications through problem back.

    4. 4、How to make medical plastic data transparent and without tampering?

      Medical institutions in HEY chain must dock our self-developed electronic medical records system. All medical data will be decentralized storage on the chain and the system will start the privacy protection mechanism. Unless there are medical problems, both doctors and patients can inquire relevant data, which can make data backtracking without privacy data leak.

    5. 5、How to improve evaluation database of consumer and medical institutions?

      HEY chain will set privacy protection by permission and used by regulators to improve the evaluation database between consumers, medical institutions, physicians, training institutions and pharmaceutical companies.
